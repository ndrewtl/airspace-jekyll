Our mission is to revolutionize the way Front end Development is taught in the code camp environment. This isn't a code camp. It's the Special Forces.

ABOUT US

Depth First Academy is a new kind of coding school experience. You can think of it as a master's bootcamp. Whether you are fresh out of a coding bootcamp and struggling to find a job or are a self-taught hacker, we want to help take you to the next level.

OUR APPROACH

In our experience, coders that are rejected from jobs because of lack of experience have very similar weaknesses that just need a little help. At Depth First Academy, we provide a rigorous production mirrored environment, where all projects are team based, and industry professionals will be code reviewing and shaping how you architect your decisions as a developer. No more thinking "Well, I got it to work, but I don't know if this is how a pro would do it."

Not only will you walk a way with a deep understanding of the technologies we will cover, we will also help teach you how to apply that knowledge to learn other technologies. We don't want to just teach you a bunch of stuff, we want to help you learn how to learn more effectively on your own.
