COMPLETE YOUR EDUCATION AND GET THE JOB YOU WANT

Our mission is to help junior coders fill in the gaps that are holding them back from their dream job.

ABOUT US

Depth First Academy is a new kind of coding school. You can think of it as a master's bootcamp. Whether you are fresh out of a coding bootcamp and struggling to find a job or are a self-taught hacker, we want to help take you to the next level.

OUR APPROACH

In our experience, coders that are rejected from jobs because of lack of experience have very similar weaknesses that just need a little help. At Depth First Academy, we provide a rigorous production mirrored environment, where all projects are team based, and industry professionals will be code reviewing and shaping how you architect your decisions as a developer. No more thinking "Well I got it to work but I don't know if this is how a pro would do it"

Not only will you walk a way with a deep understanding of the technologies we will cover, we will also help teach you how to apply that knowledge to learn other technologies. We don't want to just teach you a bunch of stuff, we want to help you learn how to learn more effectively on your own.

HOW IT WORKS

Code Like A Pro

Professional developers give feedback on every piece of code you write.

Remote

Depth First Academy is 100% remote, so you can become a master coder from the comfort of your own home.

Projects

Work on both individual and team projects.

Dev Tools

Become proficient in the tools of the trade (profiling, debugging, etc)

Depth

Become an expert by going in depth on the carefully chosen tech stack.

Scale

Gain experience working on a large application, like you would in the real world.

Pair Programming

Learn how to work with other people and communicate effectively through pair programming.

Interview Preparation

Develop the skills and knowledge you need to nail the interview.
